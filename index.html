Twin Village: История и задания (Уровень Elementary)

Диалог

Anna: Hello, Ben. Look at my travel plan. I want to visit Kodinhi in south India.

Ben: Kodinhi? Why our trip to Kodinhi? What is special about this place?

Anna: It’s a very interesting place. Its nickname is 'Twin Village'. There are many, many twins there!

Ben: Oh, really? Is that normal?

Anna: No, it is not normal. The text says there are 290 families. And their babies are often twins. In one family, there are seven boys! Four of them are twins.

Ben: Wow! Their parents must be very busy! Do you have a sister or a brother?

Anna: Yes, I have one sister. Her name is Kate. She is a teacher. What about you, Ben?

Ben: I have one brother. His name is Tom. He lives in Canada with his family. Our parents live here with us.

Anna: That’s nice. So, Kodinhi is a great idea. We can ask people about their lives and their families.

Ben: Okay, let’s go! I think it's a wonderful plan for us.

Словарь (важные слова)
twin — близнец
village — деревня
family — семья
wife / husband — жена / муж
children — дети
2. Задания на понимание и грамматику

Задание A: True or False (Правда или Ложь)

Прочитайте историю еще раз и решите, верны ли утверждения (True) или нет (False).

Задание B: Complete the Sentences (Заполните пропуски)

Используйте притяжательные местоимения: their, our, his, her, its, my.
